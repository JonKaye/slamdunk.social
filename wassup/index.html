What is this?

YOUR LEAGUE. YOUR PICKS. YOU RULE.

SlamDunk Social is a new prediction app for taking the game beyond your match days. Pick who you think is going to win — then come match day, watch your prediction play out in real time.

No real money. No gambling. Just 💪 Flex — virtual currency you earn by calling games right, and even just for playing in games. The Flexboard is where you prove you know ball, and so will everyone else.

You can also trash talk opponents before your match, or fist bump other players in support. It's the group chat, but with stakes.

Your next match

SEE YOUR ODDS BEFORE TIP-OFF

The home screen shows your upcoming match with live odds based on team records and the market pulse. The market knows your history — if you're the underdog, a win pays more. The line moves as more picks come in, so lock in early to get better odds.

Make your picks

BET ON ANY MATCH IN THE LEAGUE

You don't have to be playing to have skin in the game. Pick winners across all matchups before they tip off. The 🎯 icon shows where you've already locked in.

Place your pick

CHOOSE YOUR WAGER. LOCK IT IN.

Tap the odds, pick your Flex amount, and hit Lock It In. Your payout is calculated at the moment you bet — odds can shift as more picks come in, so early calls pay better.

Not just for players

BRING YOUR FANS INTO THE GAME

You don't have to be on a team to play. Spouses, friends, regulars who come to watch — anyone with a rooting interest can make picks and compete on the Flexboard.

Invite anyone who follows the league. The more people predicting, the more the odds move, and the more you demonstrate your picking prowess when you call it right.
